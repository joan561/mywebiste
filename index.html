Best Christian Content creators for Christian Teens

Introduction

As a Christian Teen, I think it's important to mind what we watch as it may affect our way of thinking and doing certain things. For a while I struggled with finding the content that I like and that genuinely satisfies my Spirit.

I have compiled 3 different Christian Content creators that encourage and inspire me:

3 Best Christian Content creators for Christian Teens:

1. Bryce Crawford:

Bryce is young evangelist and podcast host based in Los Angeles, California. After a transformative encounter with Jesus, he committed to full-time ministry, focusing on spreading the Gospel and fostering authentic faith communities. 

The more I watched his videos, the more I grew closer to the Lord as a young teenage girl. The way he analyses the Bible and genuinely loves the Lord is really encouraging.

2. Ally Yost

Ally is a female christian content creator and a podcast host called "Christ with coffee on ice." She is also based in Los Angeles, she is known for her emotionally honest and faith-driven content. Her journey into digital influence began with a deep desire to share her personal growth and relationship with God. 

Her podcast is really inspiring and honest, I love how she really shows her vulnerability in her podcast whenever it comes to salvation. Her podcast is really relatable especially for girls and i think that is what makes it really personal and comforting.  

3. Julia Poe Peterson

Julia is a Christian content creator known for sharing faith-rooted parenting moments, marriage insights, and spiritual encouragement across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. She is happily married to Jacob Petersen. Julia also makes 

Her content on her daily routines as a young christian mom, inspire me to incorporate time in my day to read the Bible, what I really love about her is the advice that she provides about relationships, bible studies, and friendships.
